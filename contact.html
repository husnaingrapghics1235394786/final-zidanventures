Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I track my order? +
All orders ship via Amazon's fulfillment network. Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking email from Amazon directly. You can also track through your Amazon account under "Your Orders."
What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy through Amazon. Simply initiate a return from your Amazon orders page and we'll process a full refund once the item is received back.
Are your products Prime eligible? +
Yes! The majority of our catalog is fulfilled by Amazon (FBA), which means Prime members enjoy fast, free 1–2 day shipping on eligible items. Look for the Prime badge on each product listing.
Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing? +
Absolutely. We work with retail buyers, gift box curators, and corporate gifting programs. Email info@zidanVentures®.com with your requirements and we'll respond within one business day.
How do I become an affiliate partner? +
We love working with content creators and bloggers. Send us an email at info@zidanVentures®.com with your platform details and audience size, and our team will get in touch within 48 hours.
Are your products safe and certified? +
Yes. All ZidanVentures® products are third-party tested and comply with relevant safety standards. Our skincare and wellness items are dermatologist tested and cruelty-free. Full certification details are available on each product page.
