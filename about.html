Our Journey

How We Got Here

Nov 2025 — Year One
The First Product

ZidanVentures® launched with a single Corn Peeler product listed on Amazon. It sold out in a single weeks — and we knew we were onto something real.

Dec 2025 — Expanding
Added More Products in Our Catalog

We grew our catalog to 5 products and hit our first major milestone — 497 verified Amazon purchases. The reviews were overwhelming, and word-of-mouth took over.

20 December 2025 — Recognition

One of our products earned the coveted "Amazon's Choice" badge. We doubled our team and launched into home & kitchen — our fastest growing category.

2025 — Scale
700+ Sales On Amazon

Now with 6 products, international customers, and an excellent average rating, ZidanVentures® is recognized as one of the most trusted personal brand sellers on Amazon.
